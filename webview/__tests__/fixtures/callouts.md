# Callouts

> [!NOTE]
> GitHub note with the body attached to the marker line.

> [!TIP]
> A tip.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Important body.

> [!WARNING]
> Warning body.

> [!CAUTION]
> Caution body.

> [!note] Obsidian style with a title
> Body text under the title.

> [!tip]- Folded with title
> Hidden until expanded.

> [!faq]+ Expanded fold
> Question and answer.

> [!abstract]
>
> Marker separated from the body by a blank quote line.

> [!custom-type] Unknown type
> Falls back to note styling but keeps its raw type.

> [!NOTE]

> [!WARNING] a **formatted** title stays a plain blockquote
> Body under the formatted marker line.

> \[!NOTE] an escaped marker stays a plain blockquote.

> Regular blockquote without a marker.
> Second line.

> [!success] Outer callout
> Outer body.
>
> > [!bug] Inner callout
> > Inner body.

Prose with [!NOTE] outside a blockquote stays prose.
